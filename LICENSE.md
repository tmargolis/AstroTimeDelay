2. ARTISTIC WORK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following elements are protected as original artistic expression and may
not be reproduced or adapted without written authorization:

  (a) The concept of using scientifically-calibrated light-travel delays to
      create time-displaced video mirrors as a form of experiential artwork.

  (b) The specific selection, framing, and scaling of delays corresponding to
      the Moon (1.3 seconds), the Sun (8 minutes 20 seconds), Saturn (79
      minutes), and Proxima Centauri (4.24 years) as the subjects of the work.

  (c) The Collector's Edition model for the Proxima Centauri mirror, including
      the motion-based best-frame selection methodology as an artistic choice,
      the concept of a mirror that accumulates a domestic record over years, and
      the pre-acquisition consent process as an integral component of the work.

  (d) The "ghost-stack" EMA composite as a visual element of the installation.

  (e) The movie barcode strip as a visual representation of buffered time
      within this artistic context.

  (f) All written materials associated with the work, including artist
      statements, the README, and the Privacy document.
